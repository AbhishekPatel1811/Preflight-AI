# Frontend Quality Bar

Launch Desk UI should feel like a premium command center for product launches:

- Strong first impression above the fold.
- Clear hierarchy and crisp spacing.
- Responsive desktop, tablet, and mobile layouts.
- Accessible contrast in light and dark themes.
- Keyboard-friendly controls and visible focus states.
- Reduced-motion support.
- Motion used for purposeful reveal, timeline, and interaction effects.
- No fake logos, testimonials, customers, funding claims, or usage metrics.
- No analytics, telemetry, or third-party tracking unless explicitly requested.
- No secrets or private launch briefs in client code.
